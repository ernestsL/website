Mac & Cheese

Ingredients:

2 cups pasta
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 cups milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper

instructions:

1. Cook the macaroni in boiling water, then drain it.
2. In a pot, melt the butter over medium heat.
3. Add the milk and heat it gently.
4. Stir in the shredded cheese until it melts and becomes smooth.
5. Add the cooked macaroni and mix well.
6. Season with a little salt and pepper if you want.

Pancakes

Ingredients:

1 cup flour
1 tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup milk
1 egg
2 tablespoons melted butter

Instruction:

1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
2. Whisk in milk, egg, and melted butter until smooth.
3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
4. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.
5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface,
then flip and cook 1-2 minutes more.
6. Serve warm with syrup or toppings.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

1 cup butter, softened
1 cup sugar
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup chocolate chips

Instruction:

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. In a bowl, cream together butter and sugar.
3. Mix in vanilla extract.
4. Gradually add flour, baking soda,
and salt, stirring until combined.
5. Fold in chocolate chips.
6. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet.
7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.
8. Let cool and enjoy!
